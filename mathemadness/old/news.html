TI-84 Plus CE Python Announced! • 6 May 2021

After 6 years, Texas Instruments has finally brought Python to the 84 Plus CE without the use of the defunct Python module. On their American site this time! Alongside the French 82 Advanced Edition Python, it will be available in Fall of 2021. On TI's instagram, We can see what appears to be a live demo of Python drawing text to the screen as a neat Star Wars reference, along with a brand new peach-like color, and a lavender color! The specifications appear to be the same as the normal, later-made CEs, and the 83 Premium CE.

arTIfiCE opens up Asm support after TI lockout • 29 October 2020

In TI's latest OS update for the 84 Plus CE and it's variants, support for unsigned programs written in eZ80 Assembly was removed.

However, a jailbreak arose just a few months later known as arTIfiCE! The jailbreak works by using a flaw in TI's Cabri Jr. Flash App, which work similarly to Asm programs, and can install launchers like Cesium into the apps menu without being signed. Huzzah!
Visit arTIfiCE's website to find out how to install it for yourself. Happy programming!
