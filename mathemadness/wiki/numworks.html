Numworks

Numworks is the name of a french company who has made a calculator of the same name. It's marketed as an alternative to the TI-84 Plus family.

At launch, one of the main selling points of this calculator was its open source nature. Both the hardware schematics and operating system, Epsilon, are available for anyone to download or even contribute to. However, since the release of Epsilon version 16, Numworks has gone back on their word somewhat. Custom firmwares are no longer able to be installed on the device due to concerns of cheating on exams.
